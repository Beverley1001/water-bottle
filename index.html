Water Bottle's Adventure

The biggest regret in life is not missing the best,

but not being able to give your best when you finally get it.


I'm a water bottle, filled with a liquid called emotion.

I have saved a dying goldfish.

But I forgot the memory of goldfish only last for seven seconds.

Until the day I let him go, he did not bring any of memory about me.

After, I met a sponge.

He was roasted into a dry sponge in the hot sun.

Then I gave water to him, and we become friend.

But I know

"Friend" not just take.

In my most difficult time, Cactus has been with me.

I came with him to his hometown, desert.

However, I gradually realized that something I regarded to life is he did not need.

If you Don't need it, then it means it is not important...

I always think about that if fate makes me met crystal sugar earlier.

Maybe I will be sweet everyday

the best, but not being able to give your best when you finally get it.
